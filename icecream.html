Homemade Vanilla Ice Cream

Cool down with this rich and creamy homemade vanilla ice cream that's easy to make and perfect for any occasion!

Ingredients:

    2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Instructions:

  1. Mix the heavy cream, whole milk, sugar, vanilla extract, and salt in a large bowl until the sugar is fully dissolved.
  2. Chill the mixture in the refrigerator for about 1-2 hours for best results.
  3. Pour the chilled mixture into your ice cream maker according to the manufacturer's instructions.
  4. Churn the mixture for about 20-25 minutes until it thickens and has a soft-serve consistency.
  5. Transfer the ice cream to an airtight container and freeze for at least 4 hours or overnight to firm up.
  6. Serve the homemade vanilla ice cream in bowls or cones and enjoy!
